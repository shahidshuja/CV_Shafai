Research profile

Research Scientist with 6+ years of applied experience in satellite-based environmental monitoring, Geo-AI modelling, and multi-sensor Earth Observation across agro-forestry, cryosphere, and water quality domains. Holds an MSc from ITC, University of Twente, Netherlands (GPA 8.06) under a competitive ISRO merit scholarship — the world's leading institution for applied remote sensing. Deep expertise in SAR/PolInSAR physics, radiative transfer modelling, and ML/DL pipelines; 12+ Q1 peer-reviewed papers; 5 Google Earth Engine applications in production; Co-PI experience on national and international funded programmes. Founder of Farm2i — India's first satellite and AI-powered Farm Management System for perennial horticulture under Himalayan agro-climatic conditions.
